Our Story

Built because Indian manufacturers
deserve to win.

Indian SMBs make world-class products. They lose deals not because of quality or price — but because they respond too slowly. We're here to fix that.

The Problem

14,000 inquiries go unanswered every day.

Walk into any steel trader's office in Ahmedabad, any textile manufacturer's unit in Surat, any chemical supplier's desk in Mumbai. You'll find the same scene: a business owner buried in WhatsApp messages, jumping between customer inquiries, supplier calls, and factory updates. The phone doesn't stop buzzing.

A buyer in Delhi sends "TMT 12mm 500kg chahiye, rate batao." The owner reads it between meetings. Means to reply after lunch. Gets pulled into a production issue. By evening, the buyer has already placed the order with someone who responded in 2 hours.

The deal was lost — not because the price was wrong, not because the product was inferior, but because the response came too late.

This happens thousands of times a day across Indian manufacturing. The average response time to a B2B inquiry in India is 10 days. Chinese competitors respond in 2 hours. The math doesn't work.

The problem isn't laziness. It isn't lack of intent. It's that the owner is the only person who knows the pricing. The owner is the only person who can approve a quote. And the owner is managing a factory, a team, logistics, compliance, and a hundred WhatsApp groups — all from the same phone.

The Insight

Don't change how they work. Speed up what they already do.

We talked to dozens of SMB owners. Every "solution" they'd tried — CRMs, quoting software, ERP systems — failed for the same reason: it required them to change their behavior. Open a dashboard. Fill a form. Learn a new tool.

They don't need another app. They need the reply to happen faster.

The owner already gets the inquiry on WhatsApp. They already know the price in their head. The missing piece is everything between "I know the price" and "the customer has a professional proposal in their inbox." That's what Pakka Quote automates.

Forward the inquiry. Type the price. Say SEND. That's your entire workflow. We handle parsing, proposal generation, PDF creation, delivery, and follow-ups.
Why "Pakka Quote"

Pakka means it's done. For real.

In Indian business culture, "pakka" means confirmed, solid, trustworthy. When a trader says "pakka deal" — it's done, no going back. That's exactly what we deliver: a professional, confirmed quote that your buyer can trust and act on.

And "quote" is the exact word Indian traders use. Not "proposal." Not "estimate." Quote. Pakka Quote — a confirmed, professional quote. The name says what the product does.

What We Believe

Principles that guide everything we build.

01
No behavior change
If it requires the owner to open a new app, fill a form, or learn a dashboard — we've failed. The product must work where they already are: WhatsApp.
02
Owner stays in control
We never send a quote without the owner's explicit approval. Pricing lives in the owner's head for a reason — we don't automate that away. We automate everything around it.
03
Speed is the feature
A quote that arrives in 5 minutes beats a perfect quote that arrives in 5 days. We optimize for response time above all else because that's what wins deals.
04
Respect the language
Indian business happens in Hindi, Hinglish, and a dozen regional languages. Our AI doesn't force English — it understands however your buyer actually communicates.
