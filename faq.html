Getting Started
The basics
Pakka Quote is an AI-powered system that turns WhatsApp inquiries into professional PDF proposals. When your buyer sends an inquiry on WhatsApp, you forward it to Pakka Quote. Our AI parses the inquiry, you give the price, and a branded PDF proposal is generated and sent to the buyer via email — all within minutes. No app to download, no dashboard to learn.
No. Pakka Quote works entirely through WhatsApp and email — the tools you already use. There's no app to install, no website to log into, and no dashboard to learn. You interact with Pakka Quote by forwarding messages and replying on WhatsApp. That's it.
Join the waitlist with your WhatsApp number. We'll reach out within 24 hours to onboard you. Onboarding happens entirely on WhatsApp — we'll ask you a few questions about your business (name, GST, products, payment terms) and you'll be ready to send your first quote. The whole setup takes under 5 minutes.
Pakka Quote works for any B2B business that receives product inquiries and sends quotations — steel, textiles, chemicals, FMCG, building materials, industrial supplies, auto parts, and more. If your buyers send inquiries on WhatsApp and you respond with pricing, Pakka Quote can automate that workflow.
How It Works
The details
Our AI is trained to understand the way Indian B2B buyers actually communicate — including Hindi, Hinglish, mixed languages, abbreviations, and industry-specific terms. It extracts the product name, quantity, specifications, delivery location, and deadline from the message. It works with text messages, voice notes (converted via speech-to-text), photos of handwritten orders, and PDF attachments.
You always see the parsed summary before anything goes to the buyer. If something looks off, you can correct it. And importantly — Pakka Quote never auto-sends a quote without your approval. You review the draft and explicitly reply SEND. If the AI isn't confident about a detail, it'll ask you a clarifying question instead of guessing.
Yes. After you provide the price and the draft is generated, you have three options: SEND to approve and deliver, EDIT to make changes (you'll be prompted for what to change), or SKIP to cancel this proposal entirely. You're always in control.
After a proposal is sent, Pakka Quote automatically sends follow-up emails on your behalf — on day 2 (warm nudge), day 5 (check-in with quote validity reminder), and day 10 (final touchpoint). If the buyer replies to any follow-up, the sequence stops automatically. You can also stop it manually anytime from WhatsApp.

One proposal = one PDF quotation generated and sent to a buyer. If you skip a proposal (reply SKIP instead of SEND), it doesn't count toward your limit. Edits to a proposal before sending also don't count extra — only the final sent version counts.

Your data, your business

We use encrypted connections for all data transfer and follow industry-standard security practices. Your pricing information is especially sensitive — it's never exposed to anyone except you and your buyer.
